WHY SEWA

Detailed Planner

Course-wise year long planner is prepared and provided to all the enrolled students in the beginning of academic session. It consists of date-wise description of various chapters along with the number of lectures & duration.

  • Lectures schedule
  • Date wise description
  • Smart class.
  • Test planning for JEE(MAIN+ADVANCED) NEET JEE(MAIN)-II / NATA

Student Performance Report

Student’s academic performance in each test is analyzed on various parameters at macro & micro level & detailed report is made available on website under student zone

  • Analysis of weakness
  • Inform parents about their Student ‘s level of achievement
  • Weekly report
  • Counselling

Mentorship Program

Our new concept of appointing one mentor for the group of every 5 students will make the students comfortable in the campus as well as make easy for the parents/guardians to get the report of their ward regularly.

Study Material

Study material covers the core concepts of a topic and has relevant illustrations which help the students to understand the basics. SEWA’s study material has earned the reputation of being the most up to date when it comes to latest changes and trends in examination patterns. Exam oriented knowledge- base covering all the topics, strengthens fundamental concepts and develops problem-solving skills.

  • Crash courses
  • Previous year question paper
  • Free video Library
  • DPPs & Exercise sheet

Regular Exam

We conduct periodic test series to judge and monitor the performance and understanding levels of students. Efforts are made to create real exam like scenario, so as to get the students acclimatized to exam situation. Appearing regularly in these tests increases student’s speed which is an extremely important aspect in all the competitive examinations.

  • Online/Offline test series​
  • Periodic test
  • Weekly test
