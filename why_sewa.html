WHY SEWA

ACADEMIC FEATURES

WORKSHOPS

Along with the target examinations, we at SEWA coordinate many National & International competitive exams, short term workshops, mock tests & various test series are arranged . Those students who are unable to join year long classroom programmes due to any reason, can also attend such workshops and can prepare themselves for exams in a short span.

CHOICE OF TEACHERS

This unique feature is available only at SEWA . Our faculty members are specially trained to understand SEWA’s academic system. We provide our students with the facility to select their teachers so that the student – teacher bond becomes strong which further enhances the result of students. We provide the same quality of teachers to all the students irrespective of their batches and students can choose any faculty depending upon their understanding & comfort.

INTERACTIVE CLASSROOM SESSION

Engaging students through interactive learning promotes independent thinking. We at SEWA focus to make the education a two-way communication process and encourage our students to think, brainstorm and not just cram a few rote concepts.

PLANNER

Course-wise year long planner is prepared and provided to all the enrolled students in the beginning of academic session. It consists of date-wise description of various chapters along with the number of lectures & duration.

REVISION AND BACKUP CLASSES

Courses at SEWA are so well planned that students don’t feel the need of any revision or back up classes.
However such classes are arranged for those students who missed their regular classes on genuine grounds with a prior approval.

BOARD EXAM PREPARATION CELL

It’s a unique program for the students that integrates school syllabus mastery and test preparations skills with personal self-study and focused coaching.
At SEWA, we believe to be always in sync with the time that is why the teaching formats we offer are best of both the worlds. We prepare the students for their board as well as the competitive exams so that the students are synchronized in a stress-free environment.

STAR BATCH

Student’s potential is recognized and then categorized in the ‘Distinctive Performers’ group which is the ‘STAR BATCH’ where they are taught in a highly focused environment. This enhances their capability and brushes up their talent to the finest levels. This batch consists of the best students & the level of competition amongst them is very high. For this batch, reshuffling within all the batches happens after every 3 tests and best 5% students are selected.

CONCEPTUAL PROBLEM SHEETS

Conceptual Problem Sheet including 12 exercise sheets with 10 – 15 questions related to the current and previous topics is provided to students quarterly. This ensures regular revision of all important topics.

CO-ACADEMIC FEATURES

MOTVATIONAL & COUNCELLING SESSIONS

Teachers have a lot to do with their students motivational level. Students may arrive in class with a certain degree of motivation, but the teacher’s behavior, teaching style, the nature of the assignments and informal interactions with students, all have a large effect on student motivation. We at SEWA make sure every student is full of enthusiasm & motivation. We keep on arranging motivational & counseling sessions as & when required.

DAILY ATTENDENCE

Database is maintained which includes attendance records and results of periodic tests. Both parents & students have access to these performance sheets.
All students are required to attend all classroom sessions and test series except under the unavoidable circumstances. Information about student’s absence is provided to his or her parent through SMS or call depending upon the number of days of his / her absence.

PARENT-TEACHER MEETING

PTM is the only platform when parents can interact with faculty members. It is conducted usually four times in an academic session. It gives parents an opportunity to discuss their doubts, concerns and queries.

STUDENTS PERFORMANCE MONITORING

Student’s academic performance in each test is analyzed on various parameters at macro & micro level & detailed report is made available on website under student zone.

ANSWER KEYS

At SEWA we coordinate almost all competitive examinations, conducted by Government & Non-Govt. organizations and answer keys of each competitive exam is placed on the website & notice boards.

ORIENTATION SESSION AND SEMINAR

Introductory / Orientation sessions have very much important as students are new to institute & unaware above the system, so to make them understand with all processes, Orientation Sessions are conducted in the beginning of each batch with the objective to make students’ familiar with the system.

WELL RESEARCHED STUDY MATERIAL

Study material covers the core concepts of a topic and has relevant illustrations which help the students to understand the basics. SEWA s’ study material has earned the reputation of being the most up to date when it comes to latest changes and trends in examination patterns. Exam oriented knowledge- base covering all the topics, strengthens fundamental concepts and develops problem-solving skills

PERFORMANCE EVOLUTION TESTS

We conduct periodic test series to judge and monitor the performance and understanding levels of students. Efforts are made to create real exam like scenario, so as to get the students acclimatized to exam situation.
Appearing regularly in these tests increases student’s speed which is an extremely important aspect in all the competitive examinations.

DOUBT REMOVAL SESSIONS

After a topic completion, we conduct doubt removal sessions for that topic. A team of dedicated faculties remain available on a regular basis to solve the problems. Back up classes are planned and arranged for specific students, according to their performance reports, to improve the performance level of such students.

HOME WORK CHECKING

Daily homework practice load is kept limited to 25 questions per subject.
5000+ Pages of detailed Topic – wise Theory Notes (For 11th, 12th & 12th Pass).
1000+ Pages of detailed Topic -wise Theory Notes (For 6th,7th,8th, 9th, & 10th).
Multi-level exercise sheets with objective and subjective questions.

INFRASTRUCTURE & FACILITIES

NOTICE BOARDS

Notice Boards are placed at entrance & outside every classroom. All kind of information i.e time-table, results, answer keys etc. are placed on notice boards and students are instructed to the check the notice boards on daily basis.

CANTEEN FACILITY

Understanding the students need & demand, we at SEWA provide canteen facility and maintain high standards of hygiene in relation to the preparation, storage and serving of food at the canteen.

CONVEYANCE FACILITY

The institute provides transport facility to the students from different localities to the institute and vice-versa.

VENTILATED CLASSROOMS

Good ventilation is essential for all and we believe that students perform better if their classrooms are well-ventilated, as it help students to concentrate more into studies.

PARKING FACILITIES

At SEWA, we make sure that all students get proper & safe parking facility.

SUPPORTING FEATURES

RECOGNITION & APPRECIATION

Appreciation and encouragement have a significant impact on student’s progress. We at SEWA, recognize the efforts of each student & reward them with cash award, medals etc.

MERIT-BASED SCHOLARSHIP

These scholarships are based on individual’s academic performance. Students are granted special scholarship on the basis of past scholastic achievements.

STUDENTS SATISFACTION SURVEY

Students satisfactory survey is the important component for the quality assurance. It is conducted at least thrice in an academic session for all batches to measure the academic performance of the faculty members individually and department wise.

RECREATIONAL ACTIVITIES

Recreational activities are crucial for good physical and mental health of individuals. Also to overcome with the boredom of routine recreational activities are arranged for the students as and when required.

SUCCESS CELEBRATION

By celebrating the grand achievements SEWAian scholars, constant motivation is given to the existing batches to achieve high ranks!
